# VEIL Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

VEIL ("we", "us", "our") provides a social reflection application where users connect through anonymous emotional resonance. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## 1. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

1. Account Data
- Email address (used for Supabase magic-link authentication)
- Internal user ID
- Alias and onboarding settings (for example daily prompt time)

2. User Content
- Reflection responses you submit (text, voice transcript metadata, sketch metadata)
- Optional shared response previews
- Thread messages with connection partners
- Soul snapshots you create

3. Derived Data
- Emotional signatures extracted from responses
- Soul map vectors and soul map metadata
- Connection depth and resonance-related state

4. Subscription and Billing Data
- VEIL+ entitlement status
- Purchase/restore status from store billing via RevenueCat
- Product and entitlement metadata (not raw card numbers)

5. Device and Notification Data
- Expo push notification token
- Platform type (iOS, Android, web)

## 2. How We Use Data

We use data to:
- Authenticate users and secure accounts
- Deliver prompts and store responses
- Power resonance matching and soul map features
- Enable connections, thread messaging, and unveil flows
- Process VEIL+ entitlement state
- Send push reminders/notifications when enabled
- Maintain security, reliability, and abuse prevention

## 3. How Data Is Shared

We do not sell personal data.

We share data only with service providers needed to operate VEIL:
- Supabase (database, auth, storage)
- OpenAI (emotional signature and embedding processing)
- RevenueCat (subscription entitlement management)
- Platform notification/billing providers (Google/Apple, Expo/FCM/APNs)

Shared response content is only visible based on feature behavior and your sharing choices.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain account and app data while your account is active or as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

You can request deletion (see Section 9). Deletion requests remove account-linked data from our systems, subject to legal obligations and backup constraints.

## 5. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards.

Data is transmitted over encrypted channels. Reflection response bodies and thread message bodies are encrypted in storage within our application stack. Limited previews and derived metadata may be stored separately to support product features you enable.

No method of storage/transmission is 100% secure.

## 6. Children

VEIL is not directed to children under 13 (or higher minimum age where required by local law). If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us to remove it.

## 7. International Processing

Your data may be processed in countries different from your own. We take steps to protect data in accordance with applicable law.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:
- Access data we hold about you
- Request correction
- Request deletion
- Request portability
- Object to or restrict certain processing

## 9. Deletion and Rights Requests

You can delete your account in-app from the Self tab. You can also contact:
- Email: howtodoprogramming@gmail.com
- Subject line: "Privacy Request"

Please include the email tied to your VEIL account for manual requests.

## 10. California and Similar State Rights

If applicable, residents may have rights under state privacy laws (for example access, deletion, correction, and no sale/sharing rights where relevant). We do not sell personal data.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will post the updated version with a revised "Last updated" date.
